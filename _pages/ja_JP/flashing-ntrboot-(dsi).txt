### 必読事項

Before proceeding, ensure you have read all of the information on [ntrboot](ntrboot)

This method requires temporary access to a Nintendo DSi that is compatible with your flashcart. This method uses the flashcart to run the ntrboot flasher `.nds` file on your DSi. This means that your flashcart must support launching `.nds` files on your DSi's version. [ntrboot](ntrboot) にあるフラッシュカードの対応表を見てください。

Note that in some rare circumstances, it may be possible for the flashing process to **brick** a counterfeit flashcart and render it permanently unusable. This is unlikely, but nevertheless only original listed flashcarts are supported. 偽造されたカードを受け取る可能性を減らすためには、評判のいいサイトを利用することをおすすめします。(例えば[NDS Card](http://www.nds-card.net/)など)
{: .notice--danger}

### 必要なもの

* ntrbootと互換性のあるフラッシュカード
* Two devices 
  + **The source DSi**: the Nintendo DSi which is compatible with your flashcart
  + **The target 3DS**: the 3DS family device on stock firmware
* The latest release of [ds_ntrboot_flasher](https://github.com/ntrteam/ds_ntrboot_flasher/releases/latest) *(`dsi` flasher; not the standard flasher)*

### 手順

#### セクション I - 準備

1. Power off **the source DSi**
1. Insert your flashcart's SD card into your computer
1. Copy `ds_ntrboot_flasher_dsi.nds` to your flashcart's SD card
1. Reinsert your flashcart's SD card back into your flashcart
1. Insert your ntrboot compatible DS / DSi flashcart into **the source DSi**

#### セクション II - ntrbootの書き込み

1. Launch `ds_ntrboot_flasher_dsi.nds` on **the source DSi** using your flashcart
1. (A) で続行します
1. (Up) と (Down) を使ってフラッシュカードを選択します
1. (A) で続行します
1. Press (A) to "inject ntrboothax"
1. Press (A) to select "RETAIL"
1. (A) で続行します
1. Select "EXIT"
